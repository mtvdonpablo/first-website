About Me

Hi, my name is Don Joseph. I recently graduated with an honors math degree, majoring in IT management. I majored in IT management because I found that the program had a mix of math, business, and computer science. Math and computer science were subjects that I was interested in and wanted to explore, while I used my business courses as an opportunity to develop my public speaking.

I realized about halfway through my degree that I wanted to work as a developer because it is a line of work that combines my love for problem-solving and teamwork. I also enjoy the moments where I have to sit with a problem by myself and work until I can solve it.

I had the pleasure of working as an automation developer for 1 year at an insurance company. I worked in 2 teams, which helped me learn what is required to develop reliable and production-ready solutions, as well as how to handle production failures. After that experience, I wanted to improve my understanding of data structures and algorithms as well as object-oriented programming. So, in the last 2 semesters of my schooling, I took 2 courses related to those topics, which culminated in developing a chess program using OOP.

My goal is to work in a team that values work-life balance and continuous growth, whether that is solving new problems or learning to use new technology.
